## FAQ

**Q: Is my location really recorded?**
A: Yes! Latitude/longitude captured at signing time for audit trail.

**Q: Can I change my mind after signing?**
A: Not recommended - signature is legally binding once 60% threshold met.

**Q: What if I lose my PIN?**
A: In production, you can reset via email. For now, contact support.

**Q: Are my fingerprints stored?**
A: No! Only authentication verification is stored, not actual fingerprint data.

**Q: Can others see my location?**
A: Only people with the signed contract. It's included for audit purposes.

**Q: What format is the downloaded document?**
A: Text file (.txt) with all signatures, times, locations, and QR codes.

**Q: Can I share the agreement later?**
A: Yes! Download it anytime from the final step or contact the parties.

**Q: What does 60% signature requirement mean?**
A: If there are 10 members, 6 must sign for contract to be binding.
